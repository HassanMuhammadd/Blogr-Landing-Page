Introducing an extensible editor

Blogr offers an exceedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: Creating content. The editor supports management of multiple blogs and easy manipulation of embeds such as images,videos and markdown. Extensibility with plugins and themes provides easy ways to add functionality or change the looks of the blog.

Robust content management

Flexible content management enables users to easily move through posts. Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories,sections,format or flow. With this functionality,You're in full control.

State of the art infrastructure

With reliablity and speed in mind,Worldwide data centers provide the backbone for ultra fast connectivity. This ensures your site will load instantly.No matter where your readers are, keeping your site
competitive.

Free, Open, Simple

Blogr is a free open source application backed by a large community of helpful developers. It supports features such as code syntax highlighting,RSS feeds,social media integration, and works seamlessly with google analytics. The architecture is clean and relatively easily to learn.

Powerful Tooling

Batteries included. We built a simple and straightforward CLI tool that makes customization and deployment a breeze, but capable of producing even the most complex sites.
